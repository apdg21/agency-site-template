Client onboarding checklist – automated, seamless, pro

replace buyer’s remorse with instant confidence
The moment a client signs a contract, they often experience "Buyer’s Remorse" if there is silence. To be a high-end agency in 2026, you must replace that silence with a seamless, automated onboarding experience that makes them feel they just hired a pro.

📋 The 5‑Step Onboarding Trigger

Step 1

Success
Email

Immediate & Automated

Subject: Next Steps: Launching [Company Name]’s Revenue Engine

Hi [Client Name],

Thrilled to have you on board! To keep our 48‑hour kickoff timeline, please complete these 3 quick items:

  • The Brand DNA Form – (Link to Typeform/Tally)
  • Technical Access – (Link to a "How-To" Doc)
  • The Kickoff Call – (Calendly Link)

Once these are in, my team starts the Technical Audit immediately.

Step 2

Brand DNA
Intake

Collect voice, assets, best sellers

  • Brand Voice: Witty & Bold? Professional & Minimalist?
  • Customer Pain Points: Why buy? Why hesitate?
  • Logo/Assets: Google Drive/Dropbox with hex codes, high-res images.
  • Best Sellers: Top 3 products by margin (we prioritise these in flows).
Step 3

Technical Access
Cheat Sheet

One‑pager / video: how to grant access

  • Add a "Staff Member" in Shopify.
  • Add a "Manager" in Klaviyo/Sendlane.
  • Invite to Google Analytics.

Pro Tip: Use ManageRecap or LastPass to share credentials securely.

Step 4

Internal
"Day 1" Workflow

Hour 1Create shared Slack/WhatsApp channelInstant communication
Hour 4Set up Shared Notion PortalOne "Source of Truth"

Step 5

Kickoff Call
Confidence

Transition from "Selling" to "Leading"

  • Agenda: Review Audit → Confirm 30‑Day Goal → Set approval expectations.
  • Golden Rule: Never leave without client knowing exactly when the first draft hits their inbox.
